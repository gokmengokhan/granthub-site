GrantHub — Support

GrantHub is a native macOS app for researchers and PIs to manage grant budgets, track spending, and generate reports.

Getting Help

If you have questions, feedback, or encounter any issues with GrantHub, please reach out via email. We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

Common Questions

  • How do I restore my Pro purchase? — Open GrantHub, go to Settings, and click "Restore Purchases".
  • How does iCloud sync work? — iCloud sync is a Pro feature. After upgrading, sign into iCloud on your Mac and your grant data will sync automatically across all your Macs.
  • Can I export my data? — Yes. Select any grant and use Export to save as CSV or PDF.
  • What macOS version is required? — GrantHub requires macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later.
